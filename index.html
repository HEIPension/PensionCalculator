Why This Portal Exists
Different HEIs have been calculating retirement benefits inconsistently, causing heavy financial loss to both institutions and employees. This portal standardises calculations under Sindh Pension Rules.

Empowering Employees
HEI employees can now know their exact due benefits without asking anyone. Transparency reduces disputes and enables better retirement planning across all 14+ institutions.

📚 How Pension Is Calculated

Formulas, rules, and eligibility under Sindh Liberalized Pension Rules 1977

Home › Guide
📐
Qualifying Service
  • Minimum 10 years for pension eligibility
  • Maximum 30 years counted for calculation
  • Counted from date of regular / permanent appointment
  • Leave without pay (LWP) may be excluded
  • Rounded to nearest year (6+ months = full year)
📊
Gross Pension Formula
  • Gross Pension = BFP × 7 × Service ÷ 300
  • BFP = Basic Pay + Increment (if applicable) + SPA
  • SPA: BPS-20=1,250 | BPS-21=1,350 | BPS-22=1,750
  • Increment only if DOR falls 31 May – 1 December
  • Maximum pension = 70% of BFP (30 years)
💰
Commutation
  • Employee may commute up to 35% of gross pension
  • Remaining 65% paid monthly as net pension
  • Commutation = 35% × 12 × Table Factor (by age)
  • Factor rounded to nearest whole year (<0.5 down, ≥0.5 up)
  • Full pension restored after rounded factor years
👨‍👩‍👧
Family Pension Eligibility
  • Spouse: pension for life (until remarriage)
  • Sons: eligible until age 21 or employment
  • Unmarried daughters: until marriage or employment
  • Disabled children: eligible for life regardless of age
  • After retirement: family pension = 50% of last pension
  • During service: family pension = 50% of gross pension

📐 Pension Calculation Formulas

Gross Pension = (Basic Pay + Increment + SPA) × 7 × Min(Service, 30) ÷ 300 Net Pension (65%) = Gross Pension × 0.65 Commutation (35%) = Gross Pension × 0.35 Annual Commuted = Commutation (35%) × 12 months Lump Sum Payment = Annual Commuted × Table Factor (by age) Restoration Date = (DOR + Restoration Years from Table) - 1 day Restoration Years = from official table by age at retirement (age 60 = 12 years) Example (age 60): DOR 14-Dec-2026 + 12 yrs - 1 day = 13 December 2038 [Authority: Official Restoration Years Table & F.D. O.M. No. F-1(5)Imp/2001]

📈 Adhoc Increases (Applied Cumulatively — During-Service Cases)

Base = 65% Net Pension (P₀) After Jul 2022 = P₀ × 1.635 After Jul 2023 = P₁ × 1.175 After Jul 2024 = P₂ × 1.15 After Jul 2025 = P₃ × 1.08 Medical Allow. = P₀ × 25% (BPS 1–15) | P₀ × 20% (BPS 16–22) Adhoc on Med. = Medical × 25% (01-07-2015) Orderly Allow. = PKR 25,000 (BPS 20–22 only)
